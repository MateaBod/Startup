Photomath

Photomath, a widely used program for solving mathematical problems with a smartphone camera, has lately begun reading handwriting alongside written text, making it the first in the world to do so. It is quickly increasing, with approximately 6.5 million monthly users as of today. He recently received funding that will allow him to continue his development and make strategic advances. The greatest domestic 'fintech startup' was Sabol's second Microblink initiative. It creates machine vision technology for use in Photomath and a variety of other sectors, including banking.
