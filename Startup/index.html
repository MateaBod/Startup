What is a StartUp?

Welcome!

About

A startup company, or simply a startup, refers to a business within its early phases of development. One or more entrepreneurs form a startup to develop an idea to a product or service for which they believe there is a future. Because startups typically begin with high costs and minimal income, these companies usually grow in one of two ways: investor-funded or debt-funded.
